GET ENOUGH SLEEP

To maintain health other than nutrition, as a human being it is necessary to take care of sleep time and get enough sleep so that body can rest properly and adequately. This is a predetermined bedtime Newborns: 16 until 18 hours/day, Kindergarten children: 11 until 12 hours/day, School children: At least 10 hours/day, Adolescents: 9 until 10 hours/day, Adults: 7 until 8 hours/day. If a person lacks sleep it will cause you to feel tired and it will disrupt daily routine.

TAKE BREAKFAST

Breakfast needs to be taken to provide energy to do daily work. This is because, it will lower the metabolic rate in the body as it has to work slower to store energy while waiting for you to top up energy through food. In addition, breakfast has a positive effect on a stress hormone called cortisol. Cortisol plays a role in helping the body use sugar and fat for energy sources as well as controlling stress.

MAINTAINING EATING PATTERNS

Maintaining a healthy eating pattern is not only intended to lose weight but also to maintain health. We need to eat a balanced diet so that we get the nutrients our bodies need to maintain health or restore levels of health that have declined or been affected. We need to consume more fruits and vegetables and reduce fat in our diet to live a healthy daily life

EXERCISE FOLLOW THE ABILITY

To live a healthy life we are required to be active so that our body has movement but not to harm ourselves. We can do exercise according to our bodies ability and we should understand our body language and stop when tired. In doing activities, we need to drink a lot of water because our body is hydrated when doing exercise.
